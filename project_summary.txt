Create Student Management App.
Need 3 types of users. 1.Admin, 2.Student, 3. Teachers.


ADMIN MODULE:
Admin is the superior user who can manage data.
Admin can add subjects.
Can list teachers.
Schedule batches.
Fix course fees.
Assign teachers.
Take reports of payments.


STUDENT MODULE:
Student is a guest at first. After registration gets an account in which he can enroll into a course.
Gets data about the courses.


TEACHER MODULE:
Teacher also is a guest at first. Can enroll as a teacher. Pick the subject.
